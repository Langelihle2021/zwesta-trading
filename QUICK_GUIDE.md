# Zwesta Trading System - Quick Guide

Welcome to Zwesta! Here’s how to get started and use the platform effectively:

## 1. Integrate Your Broker
- Go to the Broker Integration screen.
- Select your broker (MetaQuotes, XM, IG, etc.).
- Enter your account number, password, and server.
- Test and save your connection.

## 2. Create a Trading Bot
- Navigate to the Bot Configuration screen.
- Choose your strategy, symbols, and risk settings.
- Link your bot to a broker credential.
- Click 'Create Bot'.
- **Note:** Regular users can have up to 5 bots. Admins have no limit.

## 3. Monitor and Manage Bots
- View bot status, trades, and analytics in the dashboard.
- Pause, edit, or delete bots as needed.

## 4. Trade Details & Analytics
- Click on a bot to see detailed trade history (including 5-minute trade analytics).
- Export reports as needed.

## 5. Troubleshooting
- Ensure your MT5 terminals are running and connected.
- If you see connection errors, check your broker credentials and network.
- For multiple brokers, make sure each MT5 terminal is installed in a separate folder.

## 6. Need Help?
- Contact support or refer to the full documentation for advanced features.
